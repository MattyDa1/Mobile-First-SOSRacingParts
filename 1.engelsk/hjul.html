SOS Beta MX Maxi rear Wheel



SOS Racing Wheels


All hubs are designed in 3D cad, and then CNC machined at one of Denmark's most modern robot-controlled production facilities.

We want to deliver a product of the highest standard, and therefore we only use the best aluminum alloys, which are also used for the airline industry. Before we start, to process a hub, everything becomes aluminum ultrasonically scanned and tested in laboratories to ensure that there are no cracks and that the quality is in order.
